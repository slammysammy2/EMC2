About Me

Hey, I’m Delphine, and I am an IAPC&M accredited coach, an NLP practitioner, and a BPS accredited psychologist. My purpose in life is to help you start fully living the life of your dreams every single day.

Born and raised in HK to a French and British household, I am no stranger to melting pot of opinions, cultures, and experiences - for which I am truly grateful. When I moved to London, the city immediately felt like home, and I proudly don the title ‘a true Londoner’ to anyone that asks. I’m also a huge fan of anything dance or music related, good food, wine, and weekend adventures.

Why coaching?

I found coaching a few months after hitting my rock bottom. I had burnt out massively at my corporate job and was bedbound for 6 weeks, my relationship had fallen apart after we had started resizing a family ring, and I had entirely lost my spark.

I was deflated, disengaged, and exhausted. I had nothing left to give. Everything I knew about myself was being challenged.

I wasn’t good enough at work.
I wasn’t good enough in love.
I just wasn’t good enough.

In October 2022 enough was enough and I quit everything – and I do mean everything. I left my job with NO plan, my city without telling my friends or family, and my life. Over the next few months, I started looking for myself through deep inner work. I went back to basics. Who was I? Did I enjoy that meal? Did I enjoy that film? How did that person make me feel? Everything changed when I focused on one key question: What gives me energy? This shift was ground-breaking.

Fast forward to today and I couldn’t be happier with my life. From having nothing left to leading a life of true abundance, joy, and laughter, helping women like you fulfil their dreams and start actually LIVING – I am so unbelievably grateful for the journey.


If you’re ready for your best chapter yet but don’t know how to get there, I’m your coach.

It’s time to start playing by your own rule book.

Stop playing the game. Define it.
