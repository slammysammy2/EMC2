My Mission

Life should happen WITH you not TO you.

Over the past 2 years of working with high-performers, execs, founders & forward-thinking organisations, one thing stands true - success comes from working on the 1% changes.

empower clients to make the most of your potential using the power of your mind, habits & behaviours - alongside low effort, high impact strategies - to create incredible change, with ease.

My Background

I'm a British Psychological Society-accredited psychologist and IAPC&M-accredited coach with a background in corporate management, specializing in Intelligence & Investigations. Within two years, I was promoted to Associate Manager, leading a team of 70 across international offices

A reformed fixer, perfectionist, and people pleaser, I now focus on helping people overcome imposter syndrome, burnout, and major life transitions.

My approach blends mindset work, embodiment practices, and science-backed psychological strategies that get real results.
